Register as an Easter Bunny supplier

Register with the Easter Bunny Agency (EBA) if you want to sell or make easter eggs.

If you have a subsidiary company, it must register separately from the parent company.

There is a one-off fee of £40 for Easter Bunny licence.

Your Easter Bunny Licence will be sent to your GOV.UK App or by post.
